Digital Literacy Checklist
Do’s
•	Always Think twice before posting anything on the internet .
•	Use privacy settings properly .
•	Respect other’s opinions and be polite .
•	Verify information before sharing. 
•	Keep your personal information limited.
Don’ts
•	Do not share fake news or unverified content .
•	Do not post offensive or abusive comments .
•	Don’t overshare personal details (like phone number, address) .
•	Don’t engage in online fights or arguments .
•	Don’t click on suspicious links or messages .
