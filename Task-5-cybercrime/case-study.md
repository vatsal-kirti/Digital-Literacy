# Digital Ambassador Awareness Resource: Phishing Case Study

As a Digital Ambassador, I chose phishing because honestly it can happen to anyone. Even people who think they are very smart online also fall into this sometimes. The worst thing is it doesn’t even feel like hacking, it just feels like a normal message, and that’s why people trust it easily.

---

##  Case Study: The “Urgent Account Verification” Scam

Imagine you are just chilling or maybe doing some work and suddenly one message comes:

> “Your bank account has suspicious activity. Click here now or your account will be blocked.”

At that time, you get a little panic and start thinking what if something really wrong happened with your account. So without thinking much, you just click the link.

It opens a website which looks exactly like your bank website. Same logo, same design, everything looks real. You don’t even doubt anything. So you quickly enter your login details and OTP just to solve the problem fast.

But actually, that website was fake.

The moment you enter your details, the scammer already gets all your information. Within a few minutes, your money can be gone and you are still thinking everything is fine.

---

##  Who is targeted

Honestly, anyone can get trapped in this. But mostly elderly people, students, or busy persons who don’t check things properly become easy targets.

---

##  Consequences

After that, it feels really bad. Money is gone, account is hacked, and you keep thinking why you even clicked that link. Sometimes scammers also use your account to message your friends and cheat them too.

---

##  Takeaway

So next time if you get any message like this, just stop for a few seconds and think. That small pause can save you from a very big problem.
